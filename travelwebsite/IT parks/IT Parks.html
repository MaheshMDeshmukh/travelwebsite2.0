1)Rajiv Gandhi Infotech Park

A 2800-acre IT park in Hinjewadi, Rajiv Gandhi InfoTech Park is one of the biggest IT Park in India. Hinjewadi has become one of the poshest and prestigious localities in Pune owing to it becoming an IT hub. There are 3 phases of the tech park with a phase 4 expected to come soon. There are many famous multinational and national companies in this huge campus like Infosys, Tech Mahindra, Cognizant, KPIT Cummins Info systems, etc. Address: City Center Building, Behind Persistent, Survey No.138/1, Rajiv Gandhi Infotech Park, Hinjawadi, Hinjewadi Phase 1 Rd, Moshi, Pimpri-Chinchwad, Maharashtra 411057

2)Cybercity Magarpatta

Magarpatta is a gated community that is spread over 600 acres and is one of the biggest and most popular townships of Pune. Magarpatta City is a self-sufficient city with amenities such as schools, malls, residential areas and more. Within this township exists Cybercity, which is an IT park. Cybercity boasts of having some of the top companies in the world such as Amdocs, Capgemini, Accenture, Deutsche Bank, HCL and Tata Elxsi to name a few. Address: Magarpatta, Hadapsar, Pune, Maharashtra 411028

One of the largest IT parks in Pune, EON Free Zone is located in Kharadi. Some of the top companies in this IT park is Sears, Mphasis, Eaton, Teito, Sunguard, Symantec, Vodafone, Barclays, Metro, Veritas and Credit Suisse to name a few. The EON IT park pune is also famous for its varied eateries and amenities. Address: EON IT Park, 1, Dholepatil Farms Rd, EON Free Zone, Kharadi, Pune, Maharashtra 411014
